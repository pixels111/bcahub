Website Purpose

BCAHub is an educational resource platform built primarily for BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) students. The platform provides:

  • Study Resources: Notes, question papers, assignments, lab records, and syllabus for all BCA semesters.
  • Content Galaxy: Articles, programs, science facts, space content, and general knowledge material.
