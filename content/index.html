How to Add Content to Content Galaxy

Send me your content in the following preferred format:

Format Requirements

  • Cover image: 2:1 ratio HD image
  • Title: of the content
  • Preview line: brief description of the content
  • Labels: related tags/categories
  • Text area: main content text
  • Images in text: any supporting images
  • Credit: source of information and images

You can either send the text directly or attach a file containing your content.
