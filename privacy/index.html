Privacy Policy

How BCAHub collects, uses, and protects your information. We believe in full transparency.

Last updated: May 26, 2025 Applies to: pixels111.github.io/bcahub and all subdomains Version 1.0 English
Our Privacy Promise
BCAHub is a free, student-built platform. We do not sell your data, show targeted ads, or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes. Your privacy is genuinely important to us.
01

Introduction

Welcome to BCAHub ("we", "us", "our"). BCAHub is a free academic resource platform built by and for BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) students. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

By accessing or using BCAHub (available at pixels111.github.io/bcahub), you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our website.

This policy applies to all visitors, users, and anyone who interacts with BCAHub including submitting reviews, using our tools, downloading resources, or filling out any forms on the site.

02

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate and improve BCAHub.

Information you voluntarily provide
When you submit a review, contact form, or feedback, you may provide your name, email address, and written comments. This is entirely optional and voluntary.
  • Name: Collected when you submit a review. Displayed publicly if you approve it.
  • Email Address: Collected only if you opt-in to notifications. Never displayed publicly.
  • Contact Number: Collected only if you opt-in to notifications. Never displayed publicly.
  • Feedback / Comments: Your written review content. Published only with your explicit approval.
  • Content Galaxy / Resource Request Forms: Messages, resource requests, links, and files (if sent via email).
We do NOT collect
We do notbcollect personal data like IP addresses or cookies. GitHub may log basic usage statistics as per GitHub’s Privacy Policy.
03

How Forms Work

All form submissions are sent directly to a connected Database using Google Apps Script. This means no third party form service stores your data.

04

Content Disclaimer

Articles, programs, and resources on this site may include:

  • Publicly available information from the internet (credited where applicable).
  • Original materials created by us (e.g., notes).

We share original notes and study content created by us and contributors. Some resources are collected from the internet. We give proper credit wherever possible. BCAHub is an open source educational project built to help students share and learn together.

05

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries: We use your contact details to reply to your message.
  • To improve our content based on feedback: Your comments and suggestions help us enhance the resources available.
  • To make resources up to date: We regularly update our materials to ensure they remain current and relevant.
  • To add new content you suggest: We welcome your ideas for additional resources that could benefit the community.

We do not use your data for advertising, tracking, or third-party sales.

06

Third-Party Services

BCAHub links to and embeds content from several third-party platforms. Each has its own privacy policy which governs their data practices:

  • Google Drive: Used to host downloadable resource files (PDFs, notes). Governed by Google Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages: BCAHub is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect server logs. Governed by GitHub's Privacy Statement.
  • YouTube: Video embeds (if any) in Content Galaxy. YouTube may set cookies when you interact with embedded videos.
  • Manabadi (SVU Results): External links for result checking. BCAHub has no control over Manabadi's data practices.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any external websites linked from BCAHub. We encourage you to read their respective privacy policies.

07

Data Storage & Security

BCAHub is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not operate databases or servers that store personal user accounts. Form submissions (reviews, contact forms) are handled through secure form services or directly emailed to the BCAHub maintainer.

  • Form entries are stored securely in our connected database and kept only as long as needed to process your request.
  • GitHub Pages servers are secure, but no online transmission is 100% risk free.
08

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information submitted to BCAHub:

  • Right to correction: You can request correction of inaccurate information in your published review.
  • Right to deletion: You can request removal of your published review at any time by emailing us.
  • Right to withdraw consent: If you approved a review for public display, you may withdraw that approval by contacting us. The review will be unpublished within 48 hours.

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at generalbca@gmail.com. We will respond within 5 business days.

09

Reviews & Ratings

BCAHub has a review system for students who have used our services. Reviews are subject to the following specific terms:

  • Reviews are manually verified before publication.
  • If you approved public display, your name and comment will be visible to all visitors.
  • Fake, duplicate, abusive, or spam reviews will be permanently deleted and the submitter may be blocked from future submissions.
  • BCAHub reserves the right to withhold or remove any review that violates our community guidelines.
10

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy at any time without prior notice. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
  • Post a notice on the BCAHub homepage.

Continued use of BCAHub after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

11

User Agreement

By using this website, you agree to:

  • Privacy Policy: By using this website, you agree to this Privacy Policy and the way we handle data.
  • Content Terms: You understand that some content is user submitted or collected from the internet.
  • Policy Changes: Regularly review this page for updates. You accept that BCAHub is a collaborative and open-source project.

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at generalbca@gmail.com. We will respond within 5 business days.

12

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy, please contact us through any of the following:

  • Email: generalbca@gmail.com
  • Contact form: Contact Us through our form
