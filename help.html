Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • For PDF Files:
    • Once the file opens, look at the top-right corner and tap the three dots (more options).
    • There you'll find the Download option. Tap it -- and you’re done!
    For DOCX (Word) Files:
    This part is slightly different.
    • After opening the file, tap the three dots, then go to “Share & export.”
    • From there, tap on “Print option” and click on PDF
    • And finally click on save option.
    Then download it -- quick and easy.
    The downloaded file will be saved directly to your local storage or Downloads folder, depending on your device settings.
