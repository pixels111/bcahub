Downloads

Once the PDF opens in your browser or Google Drive:

1
Tap the three dots (⋮) in the top-right corner of the PDF viewer.
2
Select "Download" from the menu.
3
The file saves to your Downloads folder automatically.

On desktop, you can also use Ctrl+S (Windows) or Cmd+S (Mac) while the PDF is open.

Files download automatically on Windows PCs and laptops for a smoother experience.

How do I download PDFs from BCAHub?

Watch this quick guide to see the download process in action!

Word files open in Google Docs or Drive. To download:

1
Tap the three dots menu.
2
Go to "Share & export".
3
Tap "Print option" and click on PDF.
4
And finally click on save option.

Then download it quick and easy.

Alternatively, open it in Google Docs → File → Download → Microsoft Word (.docx).

The downloaded file will be saved directly to your local storage or Downloads folder, depending on your device settings.

Add to Home Screen (App Access)

Follow these steps on your Android phone (Chrome browser):

1
Open BCAHub in Chrome browser.
2
Tap the three dots (⋮) in the top-right corner.
3
Select "Add to Home screen".
4
A popup appears — tap "Add" to confirm.
5
Tap "Add to Home screen" again if another prompt appears.
6
Go to your home screen — you'll see the BCAHub icon like an app!
7
Done! You can now open BCAHub anytime with a single tap — fast and app-like!

On iPhone (Safari): tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen" → "Add".

Reporting Issues

Report it using the Broken Links form. Include the resource title and the broken URL. We'll fix it as soon as possible!

Use the Incorrect Info or Spelling Errors form. Mention the exact text, its location on the page, and the correct version. We'll correct it promptly!

Use the Add Resources form. Mention the subject name, semester, and resource type (e.g., "Please add Python Notes for 2nd Semester"). We review all requests and add them as soon as possible.

Tell us which file, what’s wrong, and we’ll review it quickly to correct the issue.

Use the Change Syllabus form. If verified, we'll update or add the changed content to match your academic year. Please attach the updated syllabus document if possible.
